The Long Dunn Game

A century of family tradition

The Long Dunn Game is a beloved family word game. You’re given a random letter and race the clock to fill in a word beginning with that letter for every category — Town, Country, Fruit, Animal, River and more. Play solo against the timer, or create a game and play live with family and friends.

How to play

  • Individual Challenge: tap to start, get your letter, and fill every category before the five-minute timer runs out. Score 10 points per valid answer.
  • Group Challenge: create a game to get a code, share it with others, and everyone plays the same letter at the same time.
