## Description  *(max 4000 chars)*
The Long Dunn Game is a beloved family word game with over a century of history — from the parlours of London to family gatherings around the world. Now you can play it anywhere.

The rules are simple and the fun is endless. You're given a random letter, and the clock starts. Race to fill in a word beginning with that letter for every category — Town, Country, Fruit, Animal, River and more. Score points for every valid answer, and aim for a high score to earn a special celebration.

PLAY YOUR WAY
• Individual Challenge — test yourself solo against the five-minute clock.
• Group Challenge — create a game, share the code, and play live with family and friends wherever they are.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Quick to learn, hard to put down — perfect for all ages.
• 14 classic categories that reward quick thinking and creativity.
• A real-time timer and live scoring that keep every round exciting.
• Elegant, distraction-free design with a warm, timeless feel.
• A genuine family tradition, lovingly preserved for a new generation.

Whether it's a holiday gathering, a long car journey, or a quiet evening at home, The Long Dunn Game brings everyone together around words, wit, and a little friendly competition.

Pick a letter. Beat the clock. Keep the tradition alive.
